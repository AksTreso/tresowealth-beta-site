India GDP Growth 2026: Why 7-8% Is Sustainable

Current Performance

8.2% GDP growth in 2024 - fastest among major economies.

Growth Drivers (3 Pillars)

  • Consumption: Rising middle class, urbanization
  • Investment: Infrastructure, manufacturing capex
  • Services: IT, financial services, exports

Sustainability Through 2030

7-8% annual GDP growth is sustainable due to:

  • Demographic dividend (working-age population peak)
  • Manufacturing boom (25% of GDP by 2025)
  • Digital infrastructure (UPI, Aadhaar, digital public goods)
  • Reform momentum (GST, IBC, FDI liberalization)
