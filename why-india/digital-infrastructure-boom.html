India's Digital Infrastructure Boom 2026

Digital Public Goods

India has built the world's largest digital public infrastructure:

  • Aadhaar: 1.3B+ digital IDs (world's largest)
  • UPI: 10B+ transactions monthly (vs 2B for China+US combined)
  • Data Protection: DPDP Act 2023 creates regulatory clarity
  • Digital Payments: 40% of digital payments globally (India-first)

Investment Implications

Fintech AIFs, tech-focused PMS, and Category III AIFs targeting digital economy stocks.
