GIFT CITY AIF Tax Guide for UAE Residents

0% Tax for UAE Investors

GIFT CITY Tax Benefits:

  • 0% long-term capital gains tax
  • 0% short-term capital gains tax (Category III AIF)
  • 0% dividend distribution tax
  • 0% withholding tax

UAE-India DTAA

The Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA) between UAE and India provides additional protection for UAE residents.

Tax Residency Certificate

UAE residents should obtain a Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) to claim DTAA benefits if needed.
