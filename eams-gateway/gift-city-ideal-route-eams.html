Why GIFT CITY is the Ideal Route for EAMs

The Decision Framework

As a DIFC or ADGM External Asset Manager, you have two paths to India allocation:

Path A: SEBI Route (Traditional)

  • 8-week registration process
  • 20% LTCG tax
  • Complex compliance (monthly reporting)
  • INR-denominated investments
  • 4-6 week repatriation timeline

Path B: GIFT CITY Route (Recommended)

  • 2-week registration process
  • 0% LTCG tax
  • Simplified compliance (quarterly)
  • USD-denominated investments
  • 48-hour repatriation timeline

The Decision: GIFT CITY Wins

For EAMs serving foreign HNIs, GIFT CITY provides:

  • 4x faster registration (2 weeks vs 8 weeks)
  • 100% tax savings (0% vs 20% LTCG)
  • 75% faster compliance (quarterly vs monthly)
  • No currency risk (USD vs INR)
  • 30x faster liquidity (48 hours vs 6 weeks)
