GIFT CITY AIF Account Opening for Foreigners

Required Documentation

  • Passport Copy: Notarized copy of passport
  • Proof of Address: Utility bill or bank statement
  • Bank Statements: 6 months of statements
  • Financial Statements: Proof of income/wealth
  • IFSCA KYC Form: Completed application form

Account Opening Process

  1. Submit documentation to IFSCA-regulated custodian
  2. Complete KYC verification (1-2 weeks)
  3. Sign investment management agreement
  4. Open investment account
  5. Link bank account for funding
