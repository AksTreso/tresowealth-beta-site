How Can Foreigners Invest in GIFT CITY AIFs?

Eligibility for Foreign HNIs

Foreign HNIs from UAE, Saudi, Kuwait, and other GCC countries can invest through GIFT CITY IFSC.

  • Accredited Investor: $1M+ investable assets
  • Proof of Funds: Bank statements, wealth declarations
  • IFSCA Registration: KYC compliance (2-4 weeks)
  • Minimum Investment: $25,000 (Category III AIF)

The Investment Process

  1. Consultation: Discuss goals with TresoWealth
  2. Fund Selection: Choose Category I/II/III AIFs
  3. KYC & Documentation: Complete IFSCA requirements
  4. Account Opening: Open investment account
  5. Funding: Transfer USD/AED to GIFT CITY
  6. Investment: Capital deployed to selected AIFs
