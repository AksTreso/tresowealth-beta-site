GIFT CITY AIF KYC for Non-Indian Residents

Foreign HNI KYC Requirements

Non-Indian residents have streamlined KYC through IFSCA vs SEBI.

Key Differences

  • IFSCA KYC: Designed for foreign investors, simpler process
  • SEBI KYC: Indian resident-focused, more complex
  • Timeline: IFSCA (1-2 weeks) vs SEBI (4-6 weeks)
  • Documentation: English common law vs Indian legal framework

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (not Indian passport)
  • Proof of foreign residency
  • Foreign bank references
  • Notarized documentation
