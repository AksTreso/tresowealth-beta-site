GIFT CITY AIF Fees & Costs Explained

Fee Components

  • Management Fee: 1-2% annually
  • Performance Fee: 10-20% of profits above hurdle
  • Entry Load: 0% (no entry load)
  • Exit Load: 0% after 1 year

Total Cost of Ownership

Typical total cost: 2-3% annually (vs 2.5-3.5% in mainland India mutual funds).

The Tax Advantage

0% tax in GIFT CITY more than offsets the 0.5-1% higher fees vs mainland India.
