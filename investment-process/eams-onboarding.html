EAM Registration Process

DIFC and ADGM External Asset Managers can onboard to GIFT CITY in 2 weeks.

Registration Steps

  1. Apply to IFSCA: Submit EAM registration application
  2. Compliance Approval: IFSCA review (1-2 weeks)
  3. Custodian Setup: Open IFSCA custodian account
  4. Fund Selection: Choose AIFs for your clients
  5. Client Onboarding: Onboard your clients to GIFT CITY

EAM-Specific Benefits

  • Quarterly compliance (vs monthly SEBI)
  • English legal framework
  • Single-window clearance
  • USD-denominated investments
