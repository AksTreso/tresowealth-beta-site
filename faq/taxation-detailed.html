Taxation FAQ: 0% LTCG, DTAA, Withholding Tax

1. How does 0% tax work in GIFT City? Is it legal?
Yes, it's legal and explicitly provided by Indian law. The IFSCA Act 2019 and tax notifications specifically grant 0% tax on long-term capital gains (holdings >1 year), 0% dividend distribution tax, and 0% withholding tax for foreign investors in GIFT City IFSC. This is a policy incentive to attract foreign capital. You're fully compliant with Indian tax laws while accessing Indian markets.
2. Do I need to pay tax in my home country on GIFT City investments?
Depends on your tax residency. UAE residents: 0% personal income tax (check with tax advisor for corporate structures). Saudi residents: 0% personal income tax (check Zakat implications). Other jurisdictions: Check local tax laws. Many countries have DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement) with India, which may provide relief. Consult a qualified tax advisor in your jurisdiction.
3. What is DTAA and does it apply to GIFT City investments?
DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement) is a treaty between India and other countries to prevent double taxation. Since GIFT City offers 0% tax, DTAA may not be needed for capital gains. However, DTAA can still be relevant for: withholding tax on interest (0% in GIFT City), business income taxation, and residency status determination. Consult a tax advisor to understand DTAA benefits for your situation.
4. What is the difference between short-term and long-term capital gains tax?
In GIFT City: Short-term capital gains (holdings <1 year): 0% for Category III AIF. Long-term capital gains (holdings >1 year): 0% for all categories. In mainland India: Short-term: 15% (plus surcharge). Long-term: 10% on gains above ₹1 lakh (plus surcharge). GIFT City's 0% rate applies regardless of holding period for Category III AIF, and after 1 year for all categories.
5. Is there any withholding tax on GIFT City investments?
No. GIFT City IFSC has 0% withholding tax on: dividends, interest payments, redemption proceeds, and capital gains. This is a major advantage over mainland India, where withholding tax can be 20% or higher. When you redeem or receive distributions, you get the full amount without Indian tax deduction at source.
6. How do I report GIFT City investments in my home country tax filings?
You'll receive annual statements from your GIFT City fund manager showing: investments, redemptions, income (dividends/interest), and capital gains. For UAE residents: 0% personal income tax means no filing requirement for individuals (corporations may differ). For other jurisdictions: Declare foreign investments and income per local laws. Keep all statements for audit trail. Consult a local tax advisor for specific reporting requirements.
7. Are there any hidden taxes or charges in GIFT City?
No hidden taxes. You pay: Fund management fees (1-2.5% annually, disclosed upfront), and GST on management fees (18% on the fee amount, not on your investment). No: capital gains tax, dividend distribution tax, withholding tax, securities transaction tax, or wealth tax. The total tax drag is essentially 0% on investment returns, compared to 20-30% in mainland India.
