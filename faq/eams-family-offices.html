EAMs & Family Offices FAQ: DIFC, ADGM, Client Servicing

1. How can DIFC and ADGM EAMs access GIFT City for their clients?
DIFC and ADGM External Asset Managers can access GIFT City through IFSCA registration. Process: Complete IFSCA registration as an investment advisor (2 weeks), establish client onboarding KYC process, select suitable GIFT City AIF products for your clients, and deploy client capital. Benefits: 0% tax (vs 20% mainland), 48-hour repatriation (vs 4-6 weeks), USD-denominated products, and quarterly compliance (vs monthly SEBI). TresoWealth provides white-label solutions for EAMs.
2. What compliance requirements apply to EAMs using GIFT City?
IFSCA compliance is designed for foreign EAMs: Quarterly reporting (vs monthly SEBI), English common law framework, single-window clearance, and simplified KYC for foreign clients. EAMs must: Maintain client suitability records, conduct risk assessment for each client, provide quarterly portfolio statements, and report material events to IFSCA. No: Indian registration required, physical presence in India, or complex SEBI compliance. DIFC/ADGM regulation remains your primary compliance framework.
3. Can family offices invest directly in GIFT City AIFs?
Yes. Family offices can invest directly: Through corporate structures (LLC, holding company), through trust structures, or as individual HNIs. Minimum investments: $25K (Category III), $50K (Category II), $100K (Category I). Family office benefits: 0% tax on long-term gains, USD-denominated investments (no currency risk), 48-hour repatriation (liquidity), and access to top-tier Indian fund managers. UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) disclosure required for AML compliance.
4. How does GIFT City compare to other offshore jurisdictions?
GIFT City unique advantages: Direct access to Indian growth (8%+ GDP) vs indirect exposure in other jurisdictions, 0% tax (competitive with Singapore, Dubai), USD-denominated (no INR risk), English common law (like Singapore, Hong Kong), and Indian government backing (sovereign guarantee). Unlike other offshore centers, GIFT City gives you: Indian market access with offshore tax benefits, regulatory simplicity (IFSCA vs SEBI), and faster repatriation (48 hours vs weeks).
5. What client servicing advantages does GIFT City offer EAMs?
GIFT City enables superior client service: Faster onboarding (2 weeks vs 8 weeks SEBI), 48-hour liquidity (vs 4-6 weeks mainland), USD reporting (simpler than INR for foreign clients), quarterly fund performance reports (client-ready), dedicated relationship manager, and custom fund solutions (white-label AIFs). EAMs can offer: tax-efficient India allocation (0% vs 20%), differentiated product access (Category III AIFs), and competitive advantage over EAMs still using SEBI routes.
